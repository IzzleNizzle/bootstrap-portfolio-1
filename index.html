About Me

Daniel Mont-Eton is a professional dancer and choreographer, currently expanding his career base with Web Development. Daniel was born in Parker, Colorado and is currently 29 years old. He enjoys playing video games, hiking with friends, and cooking on the weekends.

He currently holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Modern Dance from the University of Utah. Daniel has performed with Nimbus Dance Works in New York, Evolving Doors Dance in Colorado, and Ririe-Woodbury in Utah. During his time with Ririe-Woodbury, Daniel toured throughtout Utah and the United States. With Ririe-Woodbury, Daniel also has toured throughout Mongolia and South Korea in a joint operation through the United States Department of State.

So what does the future look like? Daniel hopes to put his new found knowledge to use for the dance world. Whether it be through helping artists create websites for their brand or by creating a company that houses within it an arts organization, Daniel hopes to bridge his two worlds together.
